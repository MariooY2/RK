About Me

Hello, I'm Riad Katrib, the founder of RK Fitness. I'm a level 3 and 4 EREPS Certified instructor/Personal trainer, holding certifications from Inspire Fitness Academy (IFA). With a decade of experience in personal training, my primary goal is to help individuals lead healthier lives and transform their lifestyles. My journey in the fitness industry began at IFA, where I acquired the skills and knowledge needed to become a certified instructor and personal trainer. Over the past 10 years, I have had the privilege of working in three different gyms, each experience adding depth to my understanding of fitness and strengthening my ability to connect with people.

At RK Fitness, I am committed to professionalism and excellence. My mission is not just about guiding you towards your fitness objectives but also empowering you to make long-lasting changes to your physical and mental well-being. I firmly believe that true fitness encompasses more than just the body; it's about feeling your best, both physically and mentally. My extensive experience has taught me the importance of effective communication and tailoring fitness programs to suit individual needs. Together, with your dedication and my expertise, we can unlock your full potential and achieve your fitness goals.
